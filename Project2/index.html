What next?


Now, you will team up with one of the people you have been interacting with,
and will be solving a set of challenging logical problems as a team. You will
be randomly assigned to one of the following tasks:
  • spatial reasoning task,
  • visual/perceptual task,
  • logical reasoning task,
  • mathematical problems task.

This problem solving activity is expected to foster engagement, improve communication
and encourage cooperation between teammates.

Some of you will be able to choose one person out of the three people as your teammate.
Some of you will be randomly assigned a teammate to work with.

The top two teams that complete the most problems will each win
$30 Amazon gift certificates.

Choosing your partner

You are randomly assigned to work on the mathematical problems with your partner. Mathematical problems are
pulled out from MCAT (Medical college admission test) math test pool.You are assigned to the condition in which
you can choose a partner to solve the math problems with. Please click on the name of your preferred partner.
