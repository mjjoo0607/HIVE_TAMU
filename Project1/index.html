Welcome to the study

This study is designed to investigate the effectiveness of an online
team-building experience. Please read all the instructions carefully.

You will complete a number of simple tasks together with other people,
with whom you will be connected via the internet.

First, all participants in the study will select personal "avatars" and
write short texts to introduce themselves.

Before beginning the tasks, you will spend 3 minutes with the other
people. During this time, you will be able to read and react to
each-other's brief introductions. During this 3 minutes,
you will be able to get to know our potential team members better.
Later on, you will be in a team with another person to work on some tasks


Specific instructions will follow.

Please write two paragraphs describing two achievements that made you stand out
among your friends. For example, think of two times when your achievements made
your friends be proud or envious of you. Please include detailed explanations.

Also, please make sure you use clear and coherent language. Research has proven that
well-written texts promotes online communication and team building. Please try to be more
professional when writing about the achievements, rather than using everyday language.
(100-150 words)



Please provide few more details about yourself

Please write down your major, and describe yourself using four attributes or personal interests,
so that we can provide the information to other participants later in the study.
You can write anything from your personality to your interests.






Group Introduction

Soon after an internet connection with the other participants is established, you should be
able to see all the other participants’ descriptions. Several other participants will
be connected with you through the internet and will concurrently work on the same task.
You will ‘meet’ and interact with the other participants through an established page.
During the interaction, you will be able to get to know our potential team members better.

Even though your interaction is minimal, please try to form an impression of the people in
the group. Try to imagine them in real life - how they might look or sound, what kind of
people they are, how you would get along with them.

The task will last 4 minutes and is very important to the study, so please be attentive, without
switching pages, or doing unrelated tasks. Questions about the other people might follow.

You can read and react to the short descriptions the other participants wrote about
themselves by clicking a "like" button, similar to the "like"
button on Facebook ("favorite" on Twitter, ♥ in Tumblr and Instagram, etc.).

You can also make comments about other people's descriptions,
by clicking the message icon near the other person’s name.

Please make sure to make at least two comments and send
them to the other participants.

If these instructions are clear, you can proceed to log in.
